Federal Protections

Key federal protections for LGBTQIA+ families include:

  • Marriage Equality: Nationwide recognition of same-sex marriage (Obergefell v. Hodges, 2015)
  • Employment Protection: Discrimination based on sexual orientation or gender identity is prohibited (Bostock v. Clayton County, 2020)
  • Healthcare Rights: Section 1557 of the ACA prohibits discrimination in healthcare settings
  • Housing Protection: Fair Housing Act protections against discrimination

Source: Human Rights Campaign Federal Policy

Family Formation Rights

Understanding your rights in family planning:

  • Adoption Rights: Same-sex couples have the right to adopt in all 50 states
  • Second-Parent Adoption: Available in many states to protect non-biological parents
  • Birth Certificates: Both parents can be listed on birth certificates
  • Assisted Reproduction: Legal protections for intended parents vary by state

Source: Lambda Legal Family Rights Guide

Anti-Discrimination Protections

Protection against discrimination in family planning:

  • Healthcare Services: Protection against discrimination in fertility treatment
  • Adoption Services: Varying state protections against discrimination by agencies
  • Foster Care: Federal guidance protecting LGBTQIA+ foster parents
  • Insurance Coverage: State-specific protections for fertility treatment

Source: GLAD Legal Advocates Rights Center
